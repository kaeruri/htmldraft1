Caesar Salad Recipe! (˶˃ ᵕ ˂˶)

A classic homemade Caesar salad recipe with homemade dressing and crunchy croutons.

Ingredients

  • 1 head romaine lettuce, chopped
  • 1 cup croutons
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/4 cup Caesar dressing
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Wash and chop the romaine lettuce.
  2. In a large bowl, combine the lettuce, croutons, and Parmesan cheese.
  3. Drizzle the Caesar dressing over the salad and toss to coat evenly.
  4. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  5. Serve immediately and enjoy!
